Welcome to Equalizer

The greatest music application for music lovers and musicians alike

Brought to you by lifelong musicians invested in promoting music of all genres and musicians of all backgrounds. At Equalizer, we provide musicians with the best tools to interact with their fans and promote new ventures. Audiophiles, rejoice! Musicians can post their songs in any file they want, so you can get the best sound possible through your ~reasonably priced~ headphones. Our algorithms, brought to you by the fabulous engineers from world-renouned Wesleyan University students, is tailor made to give you accurate mind-blowing recommendations to enchance your musical palate.

Features we are developing:

  • Rating system for songs
  • Proprietary Equalizer
  • Advanced Algorithm for Song and Artist Suggestions
  • Adaptable Audio Filetypes: Uploading and Downloading
  • Artist-Fan Interactions Through Messaging Systems and Release Notifications
